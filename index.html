About

Welcome to the online gallery of Mattias Ekerhorn, a modern artist who creates stunning oil paintings that explore the themes of identity, emotion, and beauty. Each painting accompanied by a poem which reveals the inspiration and meaning behind the artwork. Ekerhorn's work is a fusion of visual and verbal expression, a dialogue between the artist and the viewer. Discover his unique vision and style, and experience the power and beauty of his art.
